About me

I am a skilled programmer, with a strong passion for videogames.
I graduated with a Master Degree in Computer Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Turin. I attended classes like Computer Graphics, Virtual Reality and Computer Animation.
During my studies, I've developed strong programming skills and deep knowledge of computer graphics, and I've enriched my experience by leading many projects.

I am now working at an IT consultancy company, while developing personal projects (games) with some friends and colleagues. I will soon release my first mobile game on the Google Play Store!
After participating at the 2019 Global Game Jam, I learned a lot about game development and teamwork, and I'm eager to learn and practice more.

Enigma

University project. The goal was to develop a game with Unity3D using Blender as modeling tool.
The game has a first-person perspective and leads the player into a small open world that puts together some of the most popular paintings by Giorgio De Chirico, such as The Seer, The Song of Love, The Uncertainty of the Poet, etc.
The goal of the game is to solve puzzles hidden among the buildings and the other elements of the paintings.

Thesis project. I collaborated with Altec SPA to develop a VR application for the visualization and navigation of astronomical data.
